About tourist attractions

Koh Mook, paradise found in Trang

   Koh Mook, a hidden gem nestled within Trang Province, is renowned for its pristine beauty. Part of the Trang Sea Islands, Koh Mook boasts fine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant shallow coral reefs. Its mesmerizing allure has earned it the title of "The most beautiful beach in the world."

   Situated in Koh Libong Subdistrict, Kantang District, Koh Mook stands out as an idyllic destination for underwater weddings in Trang Province. The island's sands are as white as silk, complemented by the gentle lapping of clear seawater in an array of captivating hues. Beneath the surface lie sprawling coral reefs adorned with brain coral, staghorn coral, soft corals, and a myriad of colorful fish species.

General information
Location

  • Koh Maa Subdistrict, Kantang District, Trang Province
  • The area on the island is divided into 2 parts: the Hat Chao Mai National Park area and the private sector. To the east are rubber plantations, coconut plantations and private resorts.
