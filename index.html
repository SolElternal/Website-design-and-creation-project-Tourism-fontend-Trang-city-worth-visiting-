Welcome to Trang

Sea Island Mountain Food Nature All in Trang

Trang Province, located in Thailand, is a place of captivating beauty and a hub of culture and natural wonders in the southern region of the country. Despite its distance, it possesses its own charm and allure that you wouldn't want to miss experiencing.

Here, you'll encounter breathtaking vistas of nature, with majestic mountains, lush forests, and stunning sandy beaches that lend enchantment to this province. It's an intriguing tourist destination with a rich history and diverse culture. Trang is also home to warm and friendly communities, where you'll find delicious cuisine and unforgettable experiences that will stay with you forever.
