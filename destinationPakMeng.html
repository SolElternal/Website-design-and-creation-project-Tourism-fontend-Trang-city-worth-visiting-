About tourist attractions

Surrounded by mountain views and pine forests. The atmosphere is quiet. Suitable for relaxation

   Pak Meng Beach is one of the beaches in Thailand. Located in the area of Mai Fad Subdistrict, Sikao District, Trang Province, it is currently under the care of Hat Chao Mai National Park. which are close to each other It is one of the famous beaches and a popular tourist destination in Trang Province. It is known as one of the first tourist attractions that people often think of and go to in Trang Province.

   Pak Meng Beach is a white sand beach with clean, clear water. It is recommended to swim in the evening so you can swim comfortably. The sand on the beach also has various shells. There is a forest of pine trees all along the beach. Makes the atmosphere shady, very cool and quiet, with cool breezes all the time, suitable for relaxing with family. Come hang out with friends and do various activities together. In the evening, sit and watch the sunset. and take souvenir photos which are very popular

General information
Location

  • Pak Meng Beach is located in Mai Fad Subdistrict, Sikao District, Trang Province.
  • It is approximately 45 kilometers from Trang city and takes approximately 1 hour to travel by car.

Travel

  • There is a van providing shuttle service from Trang town to Pak Meng Beach. Travel time is approximately 1 hour.
  • Take Highway 4 (Phetkasem) towards Trang city. Then take Highway 4044 through Sikao District. to Pak Meng Beach

Opening and closing times
Pak Meng Beach is a public beach. Therefore it is open 24 hours a day and can be visited for free. There is no admission fee.
Note
Pak Meng Beach is a relatively quiet beach.

Suitable for those who want to relax and experience nature.

Be careful of the dangers of sea waves.

Trash should be collected and the beach kept clean.
