Wang Pha Mek, Sea of Mist in Trang

   Wang Pha Mek is a viewpoint located in Ban Thung Luang, Wang Maprang Subdistrict. Wang Wiset District, Trang Province has a height of 1,600 meters above sea level. You can see the view of the sea of mist on the mountaintop. It's beautifully done. Also included are various statues. Located at the foot of the mountain, there are dinosaurs, elephants, tapirs, local fish, including the Piao crab, which is a resident crab of Wang Pha Mek. That's not all yet. Because there are also sculptures related to rubber, Luk Saba, Luk Sato, Luk Niang, as well as an artificial waterfall area. It is very beautiful and spectacular as well.

   The beautiful sea of mist that we've seen is mostly only in the northern region, right? But the southern region is not ordinary either. Trang Province also has a beautiful place at Wang Pha Mek. Let's see how beautiful the sea of mist here is.

General information
Location

  • Village No. 7, Wang Maprang Subdistrict Wang Wiset District, Trang Province

Travel

  • The first round will open and begin walking up at 4:30 a.m. The sea of fog will be there from 5:30 a.m. until around 9:00 a.m.

Facilities

  • restaurant
  • store

Business hours
Opening and closing times
Wang Pha Mek is open all year round, 24 hours every day.

As for the journey up to the viewpoint The first round will be opened and you'll start walking up at 4:30 a.m. with an official leading the way. It will be a high and steep distance of about 600 meters. It takes about 1 hour to walk. The sea of mist will be there from 5:30 a.m. until around 9:00 a.m.

Note
And every walk to the viewpoint must be accompanied by a staff member. You are strictly prohibited from going up by yourself. And you should bring a flashlight and drinking water. The walking path is dark and steep, quite slippery. In some parts of the path that are very steep, there will be ropes to hold on to at times.

In the evening, you can watch the sunset. Importantly, at the top there will be Skywalk Bridge The wooden model is a viewing point so be prepared as well.
