General information
Location

  • Koh Ngai is located in the Mu Koh Lanta National Park, 4 kilometers from Pak Meng Pier. In Trang Province, approximately 18 kilometers.
  • It takes approximately 45 minutes by boat. Koh Ngai is a small island with fine white sand beaches and clear waters, suitable for snorkeling and relaxing.

Travel

  • Passenger boat from Pak Meng Pier, Trang Province, travel time approximately 45 minutes.
  • Long-tail boat from Koh Lanta Travel time is approximately 30 minutes.
