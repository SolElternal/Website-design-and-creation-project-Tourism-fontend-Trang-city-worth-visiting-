About tourist attractions

Lao Liang Island Another island worth visiting in the Trang sea, beautiful sea water, clear sky, peaceful atmosphere. Another Thai sea treasure worth checking out.

   Koh Lao Liang is a private tourist island. Therefore, the number of tourists on the island is not very large. Therefore, anyone who wants to go to the beach in a peaceful and private atmosphere. Koh Lao Liang is definitely your answer.

   Including the underwater world of Lao Liang Island, it is beautiful beyond description. Because it's not that much if we just swim out of the beach. You will find many corals. There are corals and small branches. staghorn coral Cauliflower coral, soft coral, sea fans, and sea anemones are also abundant.
