About tourist attractions

This place is famous for its white sand beaches and clear waters, suitable for snorkeling and viewing coral reefs and aquatic life.

   Koh Libong is considered to be the largest island in Trang Province. Located in Koh Libong Subdistrict, Kantang District, it is also a source of sea grass, food for dugongs. The largest of the country as well. It is also a place to see various types of sea birds. And with the beauty of the scenery here So it's like a paradise on a sea island. For people who love the sea.

   On Koh Libong, there are a total of 3 villages: Ban Phrao, Ban Batu Puteh, and Ban Lang Khao. Most of the villagers mainly work in fishing, rubber plantations, and fruit orchards. Traditional way of life and culture Simple and unique It can be said that it is another charm that must be discovered on this island.

General information
Location

  • Koh Libong is a medium sized island. Located in the Andaman Sea. Away from the coast of Trang Province.
  • Approximately 40 kilometers in Kantang District.

Travel

  • Passenger boat from Hat Yao Pier, Kantang District, travel time approximately 30 minutes.
  • Speed boat from Kantang Pier Travel time is approximately 15 minutes.

Popular activities

Koh Libong has many beautiful snorkeling spots such as Koh Lao Lading, Koh Muk, Koh Kradan, and Koh Waen. Visitors can snorkel or scuba dive to see colorful corals and marine life.

Most Popular

Diving

Koh Libong has many beautiful beaches such as Phra Ae Beach, White Sand Beach, and Hin Khao Beach. Tourists can swim in the sea, sunbathe or relax on the beach.

Most Popular

Swimming

On Koh Libong, there are many temples such as Koh Libong Temple, Laem Son Temple, and Khao Phlai Temple. Tourists can go to worship the Buddha statue and admire the architecture of the temple.
