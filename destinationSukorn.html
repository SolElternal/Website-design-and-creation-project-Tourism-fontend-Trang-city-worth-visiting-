About tourist attractions

Fine white sand, clear water: Koh Sukon has many beautiful beaches.

   Go to the beach in one of It's better to travel to Trang. This island can be said to be a very diverse island. Whether it's the sea, forests, mountains, communities, or fruit and vegetable gardens, it's all there. We will be able to experience all of this through the community's way of life. This island is called Koh Sukorn.

   Koh Sukorn or Koh Pig is located in Palian District, Trang Province. It is a large island that is not far from the coast. The island is filled with rice fields. and green nature Including the view of the sea, it is equally beautiful. which is considered to have great natural diversity

General information
Location

  • Koh Sukorn is located in Palian District, Trang Province.
  • It is approximately 17 kilometers from Kantang Pier, taking approximately 45 minutes to travel by boat.

Travel

  • Passenger boat: There are passenger boats departing from Kantang Pier to Koh Sukorn 2 times a day. Travel time is approximately 45 minutes.
  • Long-tail boat: You can rent a long-tail boat from Kantang Pier to Koh Sukon. Travel time is approximately 30 minutes.
