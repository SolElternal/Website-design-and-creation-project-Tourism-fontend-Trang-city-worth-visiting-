About tourist attractions

Koh Chueak is two small limestone islands located between Koh Muk and Koh Kradan. In Hat Chao Mai National Park, Trang Province

   Ko Chueak is located in Ko Libong Subdistrict, Kantang District, Trang Province. It is a small rocky island. which is between Koh Muk and Koh Ngai About 12 kilometers from Pak Meng Pier is a small rocky island. that has no beach This is the most beautiful snorkeling spot in the Trang Sea. It's like the legacy of the sea.

   The highlight of “Koh Chueak” is diving while holding a rope. climb the rope See the beauty under the sea Beginner divers can also admire the underwater world. You can admire its beauty by just diving down a few meters. We were able to take beautiful photos underwater like we've seen before. It was a lot of fun. Let me tell you.

General information
Location

  • Koh Chueak is located between Koh Muk and Koh Ngai. Approximately 12 kilometers from Pak Meng Pier.
  • Located in Hat Chao Mai National Park, Trang Province.

Travel

  • By boat: Most tourists use the One Day Tour package from Pak Meng Pier. Travel time is approximately 45 minutes - 1 hour.
  • Long-tail boat: suitable for travelers who want privacy. Travel time is approximately
    30 minutes - 45 minutes.

Business hours
Opening and closing times
Koh Chueak has no fixed opening hours. Tourists can travel every day. But it must depend on the weather and sea waves.

Tours generally depart from Pak Meng Pier in the morning around 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM and arrive back around 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM.

Popular activities

Koh Chuek is well known for its clear waters and beautiful coral reefs. It's a great place to snorkel to see colorful corals and marine life.

Most Popular

Snorkeling

For divers who like adventure Koh Chueak also has many amazing scuba diving spots. Here you can find various types of fish, soft corals, staghorn corals. and even sea turtles

Most Popular

If you are looking for a more relaxing activity Kayaking is a great way to explore the island's coastline and enjoy the beautiful scenery.
